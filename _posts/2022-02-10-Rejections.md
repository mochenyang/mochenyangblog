They say you can learn more from failures than from successes. But that doesn't seem to be the case when it comes to publishing academic papers. While successes are often widely celebrated, failures are rarely publicized. In this blog post, I would take the less-traveled path and talk about the four rejections that our [ForestIV paper](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3339983) received before being accepted at XYZ. In doing so, I hope to (1) put a lid on the depressing experience for myself and (2) share some (cynical) reflections.

# The Saga

Stealing from my coauthor on this paper [Edward](https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/profile.aspx?facId=772797), the journal of publishing this paper was truly a "saga" that I've never experienced before, and hope never to experience again in the future.
